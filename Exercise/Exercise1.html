5. Explain, in a couple of paragraphs, what you think are two major challenges around DNS configuration for less-technical customers hosting websites.

There are multiple challenges someone can face while dealing with DNS configuration. From delays, cache issues, misconfiguring DNS records, the security risks and susceptibility, experiencing downtime, configuration and many more. But one of the major challenges that a user can face is the propagation delays a user will inevitably experience. This is the time that a change will reflect to a domain before reaching the internet or the end-user of the software or website. The delay is caused by the communication to DNS servers throughout the internet and its dependency on the DNS cache’s efficiency .

The second major challenge is the security risks of DNS and understanding them. It is susceptible to attacks in forms of spoofing and DDoS attacks that are mainly targeted towards the DNS servers. The effects includes having the users redirected to fake websites with malicious intent, disrupting your website’s services and acquire sensitive data from the DNS.

7. How would you set up an http 301 status redirect from “/netlify/anything” on your site, to https://www.google.com/search?q=anything. Please provide the redirect formatting here. Now, how about a proxy redirect? Please add that proxy redirect rule directly to your site.

To set up an http 301 status redirect from “/netlify/anything” on my site to https://www.google.com/search?q=anything, I would use “/netlify/:anything https://www.google.com/search?q=:anything 301!” to the redirect file.. For a proxy redirect I would use “/netlify/:anything https://www.example.com/:anything 200” to the redirect file.
